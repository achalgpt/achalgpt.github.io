The Eye Behind the Lens

Photography, for me, is a quiet pause in a world that rarely stands still - a chance to truly see. It's about catching those fleeting, delicate moments where light, shadow, and time gently align and whisper something only stillness can hear.

I'm drawn to patterns - both the kind etched into circuits and the kind scattered across a night sky. That same curiosity guides my lens, always searching for the overlooked, the in-between, the moments that speak in subtle tones.
