About The Project

Cars4Mars is our student-led entry into the 2025 African Rover Challenge—a cutting-edge robotics competition where university teams from across the continent engineer semi-autonomous planetary rovers designed to operate on Martian terrain.

Our mission? To design, build, and test a rugged planetary rover from scratch, under tight resource and time constraints. The rover must tackle a series of real-world tasks—terrain traversal, object manipulation, autonomous navigation—all on dusty, Mars-like terrain. Think NASA, but DIY, and on a student budget.
