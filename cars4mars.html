About The Project

Cars4Mars is a 6-wheeled Mars terrain rover designed and constructed by students of the UCT Space Society for the African Rover Challenge 2025.

Built for science, engineering excellence, and planetary exploration readiness, this rover represents the cutting edge of student-led space technology development in Africa.
